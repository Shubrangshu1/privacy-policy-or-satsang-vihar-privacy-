We respect your right to privacy and control over your personal data. If you wish to delete your account and associated data from the Satsang Vihar Hyderabad app, please follow the instructions below.

How to Request Account Deletion

Follow these steps to request account deletion:

  1. Email us at: info@satsanghyderabad.org.in
  2. Subject line: "Account Deletion Request - Satsang Vihar Hyderabad App"
  3. Include the following information:
    • Your full name (as registered in the app)
    • Email address associated with your account
    • Reason for deletion (optional)
  4. Our team will process your request and send you a confirmation email within 7 business days

What Data Will Be Deleted?

Upon account deletion, the following data will be permanently removed from our systems:

  • Personal Information: Your name, email address, and profile photo
  • Authentication Data: Your Google Sign-In credentials and account access
  • App Activity: Your app usage history and preferences
  • Account Status: Your account will be permanently deactivated

What Data May Be Retained?

For legal, compliance, and record-keeping purposes, the following data may be retained:

  • Donation Records: Information about donations made through the app (for tax and accounting purposes)
  • Transaction History: Records of financial transactions (required by law for audit purposes)
  • Legal Obligations: Any data required to be retained under applicable laws and regulations

⚠️ Important: Donation records may be retained for up to 7 years as required by tax and accounting regulations in India. However, your personal account access and profile information will be deleted immediately.

Processing Timeline

  • Acknowledgment: Within 2 business days of receiving your request
  • Account Deactivation: Within 7 business days
  • Data Deletion: Complete deletion within 30 days (except legally required records)
  • Confirmation: You will receive an email confirmation once deletion is complete

Can I Delete Specific Data?

Yes! If you don't want to delete your entire account but want to delete specific data, you can:

  • Email us with your request specifying what data you want removed
  • We will review your request and delete the data if it's not legally required to be retained
  • You will receive confirmation within 7 business days

After Account Deletion

Once your account is deleted:

  • You will no longer be able to sign in to the app
  • Your data will be permanently removed from our active systems
  • If you wish to use the app again, you will need to create a new account
  • Previous donation records may still be retained for legal compliance
